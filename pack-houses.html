Pack House

A Pack House is an essential structure for agricultural and horticultural operations. It serves as a centralized space for sorting, cleaning, grading, packing, and storing produce before it reaches the market. Our custom-designed pack houses are durable, well-ventilated, and tailored to suit a variety of post-harvest needs.

  • Efficient layout for post-harvest operations
  • Ventilation and insulation options for temperature control
  • Suitable for fruits, vegetables, flowers, and grains
  • Customizable space and utilities for washing, grading, and packing
